ABOUT THE APP

This application was developed in order to give a means for ITMS commanders to have easier access to various documents made by the ITMS divisions through their moble phones. The app's primary purpose is to deliver mobility and flexbility for our commanders in checking and reading documents submitted by ITMS divisions whenever and whereever they may be.

Vision

A highly capable and innovative service that delivers first-class IT solutions for the PNP.

Mission

The ITMS shall develop, implement and maintain reliable, robust and secure information systems, databases and computer networks.
